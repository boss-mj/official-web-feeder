Capabilities

Hardware Meets Software

Designed specifically for our custom ESP32 Feeder Hardware. The app provides a seamless bridge between your phone and your farm.

Laser Precision

Integrated VL53L0X laser sensor support accurately measures the physical distance of your feed to calculate exact kilogram weights.

Water Monitoring

Never let your flock go thirsty. Real-time monitoring of water reservoirs with instant push notifications when empty.

Automated Schedules

Set up complex daily feeding schedules. The ESP32 stores them locally on its RTC, meaning it feeds even if the internet drops.

Dual Connectivity

No Wi-Fi at the coop? No problem. Connect locally via Bluetooth (BLE) to configure settings or dispense feed manually.

Cloud Analytics

Powered by Firebase. View historical feeding data, monitor consumption trends, and forecast when you need to buy more feed.

Emergency Control

Instantly halt the motorized auger from anywhere in the world with the red Emergency Stop button.

Setup Guide

How to Install the APK

Because this app interfaces with custom farm hardware, it is distributed directly rather than through the Google Play Store. Follow these steps to install.

1

Download the File

Tap the orange Download button below to save the smart-feeder-v1.0.apk file to your Android device.

2

Allow Unknown Sources

Open the downloaded file. Android may show a security prompt. Tap Settings and toggle on "Allow from this source".

3

Install & Connect

Tap Install. Once finished, open the app, create an account, and use the "Connect Locally" button to pair your ESP32 hardware via Bluetooth for the first time.
